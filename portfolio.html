theta waves (2020)

York Dance Ensemble 2019/20
Choreographer: Nina Milanovski

Through a desire to depict the absurdity and emotional honesty within dreams, theta waves investigates the vivid and whimsical universe we encounter when asleep. Episodic in nature, this work takes to task the embodiment of each of the six performers' most persistent dreams and nightmares.
