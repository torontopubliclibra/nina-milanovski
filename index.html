Hello!

My name is Nina Milanovski and I am a dance artist and producer based in Tkaronto, Canada. I choreograph, perform, and I am developing my skills as a curator and presenter through community building events. Outside of my work as an artist, I am a coffee enthusiast, amateur bartender, and loving owner of my beagle Benny. If you want to keep up to date with performances, events, and calls for submissions, please follow me on Instagram.
